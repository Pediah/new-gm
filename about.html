ABOUT US GM GAMOO PSYCHOMETRISTS

We provide professional psychometric assessments for individuals and businesses, delivering clear, objective insights to support career decisions and hiring processes. For individuals, we offer career guidance, subject choice, and psycho-educational assessments, helping students and professionals navigate their academic and career paths with confidence.

Our evaluations assess intellectual aptitude, career interests, personality traits, and values to guide informed decisions about studies, career changes, and personal growth. For businesses, we conduct pre-employment psychometric testing to help employers make strategic hiring decisions. By assessing personality, integrity, cognitive abilities, and workplace values, we ensure that companies select candidates who align with their roles and organizational culture
