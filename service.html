  • Psycho-Educational Assessments – Comprehensive academic evaluations.

Business Services

We help businesses hire the best talent by providing specialized pre-hire psychometric assessments for companies of all sizes, offering objective insights into a candidate’s suitability for a specific role, helping employers make informed hiring decisions.

  • Personality & Integrity Testing – Evaluating traits relevant to job performance.
  • Cognitive Abilities Assessment – Measuring problem-solving and decision-making skills.
  • Counterproductive Workplace Behavior Profiling – Identifying potential risks in workplace behavior.
  • A professional, structured assessment environment for recruiters and companies.
